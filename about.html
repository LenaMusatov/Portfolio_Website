About Me

Hello, I'm Lèna Musatov. From a young age, I have traveled a lot and moved frequently, I can adapt quickly and easily learn new things. I have been inspired by animated movies since I was a kid. I love to build stories and characters. I have been illustrating stories since 2017, but how amazing would it be if your creations can move and come to life! Likewise, I aspire to become an animator to bring my own and other's people creations to life.
