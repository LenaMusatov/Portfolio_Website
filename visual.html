Contextual Concepting

Year 1 - CMD

In the first year of CMD (Communication & Multimedia Design), we developed and designed a dynamic visual identity based on a visual concept. The assignement was to creat this for TEDx Delft, with the theme "Let's Make."
This dynamic visual identity needed to be captured in a style guide. Additionally, the design needed to be translated into at least three different prototypes: two posters, one landing page of a website, and one or more self-selected media.

I decided to go with layers. The different layers of research that stock upon another. Float around each other and combine. This represents the information we know, take for example math, when you know how to do addition and subtraction you can calculate more difficult things, and once you know that, you can calculate even bigger operations.

Visual Design

Year 1 - CMD

In the first year of CMD (Communication & Multimedia Design), during block 4 in the course "Visual design",We had to visualize our vision of the city or village you live in. How do you see and experience your place of residence? What do you find characteristic about your place of residence? Is it a great place or not at all? What do you wish for the future of this city or village?
We got the assignment to create a series of two posters where we discover what our vision of our place of residence is.
Den Bosch was like a fresh start for me. I've been living in Den Bosch for about two months now. My first impression of the city is that it's beautiful and cozy. The buildings are colorful and the people are very nice. I used to live on the Canary Islands, where I looked at the sea every day. Den Bosch has many rivers and canals, which makes me feel at home. I designed a combination in which the sea meets the rivers and canals of Den Bosch. I am in the rivers of Den Bosch, taking a dive forward into life.
